Our story

HummingYard is an online landscape design service that works with you to bring your dreamscape to life. With 40-plus years of experience, we turn your vision into a stunning space reflecting your needs and desires, personally tailored to your style and budget. Our talented designers will partner with you to turn a lackluster outdoor space into an essential part of your home that will last a lifetime.

How it works

1. share your vision

You’ll share everything with us on a video conference call to kick off the project – your budget, design needs, site conditions, and what you envision for the space.

2. It’s All in the Details

Once provided, we’ll use your photos, videos, site plan and our 3D-design software to measure the space.

3. Co-Design Time

Incorporating your vision, our team designs the space and refines it based on your feedback to ensure it’ll meet your needs.

4. Finalizing Your Customized Space

Based on your feedback, the final design will be drawn up in a detailed layout. After connecting with our vetted network of topnotch contractors, you’ll soon be living with the landscape of your dreams.
